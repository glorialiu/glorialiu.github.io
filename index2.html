11.11.2016

I spent the summer after my sophomore year living in Folsom, a suburb outside Sacramento. I was working as a software engineering intern at Intel. As my first internship, it was a summer full of new experiences - I learned an incredible amount from my very knowledgeable team, the other interns, and also from the Windows drivers books I spent hours poring over.
